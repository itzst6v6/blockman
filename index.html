The Full, Unfiltered Story.

This isn't some noble project. This is the result of a complete mess that I had a part in. In some ways, this whole thing has ruined my life, and I'm done with it.

It started with trades I made for apps. Then my friend, Gytis, gave me the server engine. He was never supposed to give it to me, and in doing so, he betrayed our other friend, Vasu. By accepting the files, I was part of that betrayal. I'm not hiding from that.

I was naive and made a YouTube video about the 2020 engine. The chaos was instant. The fallout was fast. I was banned from Vasu's Discord project, 'BlockyVerse', which was his own server for the 2020 engine. That was the final straw.

The whole problem was that these engines were rare. That rarity is what people used for power. After the ban, I decided to destroy the one thing they cared about: the secrecy. I turned my server into a No Rules, Free Admin Land, and leaked the conversations back to Vasu. Now, I'm ending it for good by making these files public. If they aren't rare anymore, they can't be used as a weapon. The power trip is over.
